Welcome to GG Engage

GG Engage empowers schools to explore the benefits of scholastic esports — boosting wellbeing, belonging, communication, engagement, resilience and pathways to future careers.

What is GG Engage?

GG Engage is an education-first esports initiative developed to help teachers and students understand the power of esports in improving participation, attendance, wellbeing and classroom engagement. Originally funded by a CommBank & Schools Plus bursary, GG Engage now supports schools across Australia through resources, guidance and community connection.

Our Focus Areas

Wellbeing & Belonging

Creating safe, inclusive spaces where students feel welcomed, valued and connected.

Digital Skills

Building communication, teamwork, problem-solving and digital literacy through structured esports experiences.

Pathways to Employment

Showcasing real opportunities in game design, casting, production, IT, media and sport-related industries.

Why Esports?

Scholastic esports is more than gaming — it is a platform for personal growth. Students build confidence, learn to fail gracefully, develop leadership skills and strengthen positive relationships with peers and teachers.

Get Started

Explore the resources in this repository to begin implementing GG Engage at your school. From setup guides to wellbeing frameworks and event ideas, everything is designed to help teachers deliver engaging, inclusive experiences.
